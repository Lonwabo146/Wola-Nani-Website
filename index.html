What is Wola Nani?

Wola Nani is HIV/AIDS NGO.

Formed with the aim of providing assistance to the communities most affected by the HIV epidemic.

Wola Nani implemented initiatives to support individuals within the local community who are living with HIV and AIDS, enabling them to manage the emotional and financial challenges associated with the conditions
